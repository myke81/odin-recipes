Pot Roast

Description

Quick n dirty pot roast when you don't eat veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chuck Roast
  • Redmond's sea salt
  • Grass fed Beef Tallow

Steps

  1. On a plate, season roast generously with salt. Leave in fridge for 24hrs on a cooling rack.
  2. When ready to cook, leave roast on counter for up to 60 minutes to let steak come to room temperature.
  3. In a large cast-iron skillet over high, heat tallow until very hot. Add roast and sear until a dark crust forms on all sides, about 3 minutes per side.
  4. Place roast in a crock pot on low, add water until almost covered and cook for 8 - 10 hrs.
