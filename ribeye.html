Ribeye Steak

Description

You'll never crave steakhouse fare again after seeing how easy it is to whip this up at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 inch thick Ribeye
  • Redmond's sea salt
  • Butter
  • Grass fed Beef Tallow

Steps

  1. On a plate, season steak generously with salt. Let sit up to 60 minutes to let steak come to room temperature.
  2. In a large cast-iron skillet over high, heat tallow until very hot. Add steak and cook until a dark crust forms on the bottom side, about 3 minutes.
  3. Flip the steak and cook, undisturbed, 3 minutes more.
  4. Remove steak to a cutting board, add butter and cover with foil. Let rest 5 minutes before slicing. Sprinkle with sea salt before serving.
